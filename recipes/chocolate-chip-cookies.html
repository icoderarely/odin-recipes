Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description: These gooey, chewy chocolate chip cookies are easy to make and perfect for satisfying any sweet craving!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars together.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop onto baking sheet and bake for 10–12 minutes until golden.
