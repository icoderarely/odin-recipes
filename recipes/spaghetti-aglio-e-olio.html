Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description: A simple, classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, chili flakes, and parsley. Perfect for a quick dinner!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (sliced thin)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente and reserve ½ cup pasta water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and chili flakes, sauté until golden.
  3. Add drained spaghetti and reserved water to the pan.
  4. Toss well, season with salt, and add chopped parsley.
  5. Top with parmesan if desired and serve hot.
